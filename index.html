About Me

Hi, I'm Nathan J. Care, a programmer who enjoys working on both hardware and software to bring ideas to life. I've worked on projects ranging from a simple pulse counter to complex systems like a state machine for a solar vehicle. I love problem-solving and learning by doing.

I'm currently studying Computer Science at Appalachian State University. I'm also the Electrical Lead on the university's solar vehicle team, Team Sunergy, where I focus on embedded systems and telemetry. This role has helped me explore sustainable energy and work in challenging engineering settings.

Whether I'm programming a microcontroller or designing a circuit, I aim to keep learning and building. I want to work on projects that make a difference, using technology to solve real-world problems.
